Lots Of Different Personalities, But All Part Of The Same Family Character and comfort, woven together by the spirit of the neighborhoods we call home. We’re a family of unique hotels all across this big, beautiful world. Welcome to our Neighborhood.

Great design tells stories. From original architecture on full display, to subtle details that invite a second glance, each of our unique hotels across the world is a celebration of its neighborhood, telling the stories of its people, places, and traditions. We curate spaces and experiences that illuminate the beauty in each of our neighborhoods — even if you never leave the hotel.

Whether soaking up inspiration in a cozy nook or soaking in the view from a rooftop pool, we welcome everyone. At Hotel Indigo you'll find inclusive environments and flexible spaces buzzing with the neighborhood's spirit. Places that restore as much as they inspire, where you can live on your own terms and create an experience that’s as individual as you are.

Installations by local artists, ingredients sourced from nearby family farms, events put on in partnership with the people that call our neighborhoods home — we celebrate the tastes and traditions of the communities we’re a part of, and work to make a positive impact on them in return. After all, it’s their stories that have helped form our own.
